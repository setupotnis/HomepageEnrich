Our Startup

Based in Waterloo, we are two Nanotechnology Engineers who are committed to making life easier for students through the use of Enrich. We believe that there should be more focus on each student and that there should be easier access to one-on-one help in order to succeed in such a competitive environment

  • Vision
  • Mission

Modern applications of technology include streamlining the process of connecting students and mentors. Through the use of our application there will be more connection between students at the University of Waterloo. Once we gain initial support, we would like to alter our business to fit other universities while simultaneously expanding highschools in the Greater Toronto Area. Hence, our vision for Enrich is very promising and has ample potential to be successful.

To facilitate the academic growth of students primarily at the University of Waterloo through the use of technology in order to be more prepared for assessments and co-op placements.
